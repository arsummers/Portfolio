About Me

I’m a software developer with a background primarily in customer service. I was an English major in college. My past education and experience working for a busy tourist attraction bring strong communication and teamwork skills.On the more technical side of things, I currently have a strong grasp of the basics for javascript, HTML, and CSS, and I’m learning more every day. I have worked with a small team to build a needs and offers webpage, which has space to expand as I gain more knowledge. I was initially drawn to coding because I like solving puzzles and environments where my curiosity will be put to good use. As a nonbinary gendered developer, I’m passionate about helping the tech industry become more welcoming for queer people and women.
